01. Discovery

While Apple Music is an industry leader, user feedback often points to a "sterile" interface. The disconnect between the vibrant album art and the plain white/dark UI creates friction in the immersion.

The Goal: To bridge the gap between utility and emotion. I wanted the interface to feel like a liquid extension of the music itself, rather than just a container.

User Research Heuristic Analysis

02. The Solution

I introduced a Dynamic Glassmorphism System. The background doesn't just change color; it extracts the dominant palette from the album art and applies a heavy gaussian blur (80px+), creating a living, breathing backdrop.

Key Interactions:

  • Gestural Swipe for "Now Playing" queue.
  • Haptic feedback on all micro-interactions.
  • Lyrics view that floats in 3D space.
